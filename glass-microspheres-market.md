<p><p>Glass microspheres are tiny spherical particles used in various industries for their unique properties. They can be categorized into two main types: hollow and solid. Hollow glass microspheres have a lower density and are used for applications requiring lightweight materials such as aerospace and automotive industries. On the other hand, solid glass microspheres are denser and are used for applications where thermal insulation and enhanced strength are required, such as in construction and oil & gas industries. These two types cater to different market needs and offer a wide range of applications.</p></p>
