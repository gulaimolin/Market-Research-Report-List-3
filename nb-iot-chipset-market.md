<p><strong>The NB IoT Chipset Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standalone</li><li>In-Band</li><li>Guard Band</li></ul></p>
<p><p>NB IoT chipset market can be categorized into three types: Standalone, In-Band, and Guard Band. Standalone NB IoT chipsets operate independently without interfering with existing cellular networks, making them ideal for IoT devices in remote areas. In-Band chipsets use the existing LTE spectrum to transmit NB IoT signals, offering a more cost-effective solution for cellular operators. Guard Band chipsets utilize unused frequency bands within the LTE spectrum to provide dedicated NB IoT connectivity without disrupting other services.</p></p>
