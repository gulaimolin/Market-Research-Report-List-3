<p><p>Textured Vegetable Protein (TVP) is a high-protein meat substitute made from soybeans. In the food market, TVP is available in two main types: Protein Concentrate and Protein Isolate. Protein Concentrate contains around 70-80% protein and is commonly used in products like veggie burgers and meatless sausages. On the other hand, Protein Isolate contains a higher percentage of protein (90% or more) and is often used in functional food products like protein bars and shakes. Both types of TVP provide a nutritious and sustainable alternative to animal-based proteins.</p></p>
