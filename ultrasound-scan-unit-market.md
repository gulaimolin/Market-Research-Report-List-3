<p><p>The ultrasound scan unit market can be categorized into three main types: external ultrasound units, internal ultrasound units, and endoscopic ultrasound units. External ultrasound units are used on the surface of the body to capture images, while internal ultrasound units are inserted into body cavities to visualize internal organs. Endoscopic ultrasound units combine ultrasound technology with an endoscope for detailed imaging inside the body. Each type of unit serves different purposes and is utilized in various medical settings for diagnostic and therapeutic procedures.</p></p>
