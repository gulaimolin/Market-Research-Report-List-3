<p><p>Intelligent CTG (Cardiotocography) markets include both External and Internal types. External CTG involves monitoring the fetal heart rate and contractions through sensors placed on the mother's abdomen. Internal CTG, on the other hand, involves placing a scalp electrode directly on the baby's head to get a more accurate reading of the fetal heart rate. Both types provide valuable information during pregnancy and labor, helping healthcare providers assess the well-being of the baby and make informed decisions.</p></p>
