<p><p>Automatic filling machines are classified into two main types: fully-automatic and semi-automatic. Fully-automatic filling machines are capable of completing the entire filling process without requiring any human intervention, making them highly efficient and suitable for high-volume production. On the other hand, semi-automatic filling machines require some manual intervention, such as placing containers or activating the filling process, making them ideal for smaller production runs or when flexibility is needed in the production process.</p></p>
