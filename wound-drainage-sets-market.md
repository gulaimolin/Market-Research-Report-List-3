<p><p>Wound drainage sets are medical devices used to remove fluids from surgical wounds to promote healing and prevent infection. There are three main types of wound drainage sets: High Vacuum Drainage, which uses a high suction pressure to remove fluids quickly, Low Vacuum Drainage, which uses a lower suction pressure for slower drainage, and Gravity Drainage, which relies on gravity to remove fluids without suction. Each type has its own advantages and is used based on the specific needs of the patient and the surgical procedure.</p></p>
