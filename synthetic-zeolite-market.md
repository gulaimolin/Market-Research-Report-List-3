<p><ul><li>A-type Zeolite</li><li>X-type Zeolite</li><li>Y-type Zeolite</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A-type Zeolite is characterized by its high thermal stability, making it suitable for applications in catalysis and adsorption. X-type Zeolite has a three-dimensional pore structure, allowing for effective separation of molecules based on size. Y-type Zeolite is known for its strong acidity, making it ideal for use in cracking reactions in the petroleum industry. Other types of synthetic zeolites include MFI-type, BEA-type, and FAU-type, each with unique properties that make them valuable for various industrial processes such as gas adsorption, ion exchange, and wastewater treatment.</p></p>
