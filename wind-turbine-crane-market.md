<p><p>The wind turbine crane market includes various types such as crawler crane and folding crane. Crawler cranes are equipped with tracks that enable them to maneuver over rough terrain, making them ideal for wind turbine installation in remote locations. On the other hand, folding cranes are designed to be easily transported and assembled, making them suitable for smaller wind turbine projects or projects with limited space. These crane types offer flexibility and efficiency in the installation and maintenance of wind turbines.</p></p>
