<p><p>Flue exhaust mufflers can be classified into two main types based on the temperature of the flue gases they handle: hot flue exhaust mufflers and cold flue exhaust mufflers. Hot flue exhaust mufflers are designed to manage high-temperature flue gases, typically found in industrial applications like power plants and factories. On the other hand, cold flue exhaust mufflers are used for lower-temperature flue gases, commonly found in residential heating systems and other small-scale applications. Both types serve the purpose of reducing noise and emissions from flue exhaust systems.</p></p>
