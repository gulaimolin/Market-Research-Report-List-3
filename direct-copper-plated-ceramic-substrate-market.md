<p><p>Direct copper-plated ceramic substrates are commonly available in two main types: Al₂O₃ ceramic substrate and AlN ceramic substrate. Al₂O₃ ceramic substrates are known for their high thermal conductivity and excellent electrical insulation properties, making them ideal for applications requiring efficient heat dissipation. On the other hand, AlN ceramic substrates offer even higher thermal conductivity than Al₂O₃ substrates, making them suitable for demanding applications where superior heat management is essential. Both types of substrates are widely used in various electronic and semiconductor industries.</p></p>
