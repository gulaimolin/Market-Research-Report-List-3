<p><p>LED through beam photoelectric sensors are available in two main market types: digital signal and analog signal. Digital signal sensors provide a simple on/off output, making them ideal for applications requiring a binary sensing result. Analog signal sensors, on the other hand, provide a continuous output signal that can provide more detailed information about the sensed object. Analog signal sensors are often used in applications where precise distance or position measurement is required. Both types of sensors offer reliable and efficient photoelectric sensing solutions for a variety of industrial applications.</p></p>
