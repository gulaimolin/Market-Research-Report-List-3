<p><p>Hybrid combiners are used in RF and microwave systems to combine or split signals. 90-degree hybrid combiners have two output ports with a 90-degree phase difference, typically used in quadrature signal processing. 180-degree hybrid combiners have two output ports with a 180-degree phase difference and are commonly used for power combining or splitting applications. Both types of combiners are crucial components in communication systems, allowing for efficient signal processing and distribution.</p></p>
