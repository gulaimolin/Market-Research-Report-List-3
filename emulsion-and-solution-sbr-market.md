<p><ul><li>Solution SBR (s-SBR)</li><li>Emulsion SBR (e-SBR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solution SBR (s-SBR) is a type of synthetic rubber that is produced by blending styrene and butadiene in a solution. It is known for its superior abrasion resistance and high tensile strength, making it suitable for tire manufacturing. On the other hand, Emulsion SBR (e-SBR) is produced by polymerizing styrene and butadiene in an aqueous emulsion. It is commonly used in the production of footwear, industrial goods, and adhesives due to its good aging properties and low heat buildup.</p></p>
