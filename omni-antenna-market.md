<p><p>The Omni Antenna market includes various types such as Monopole Antenna, Dipole Antenna, and others. Monopole Antennas consist of a single vertical conductor, while Dipole Antennas have two conductors aligned parallel to each other. Other types of Omni Antennas may include Yagi Antennas, Loop Antennas, and Whip Antennas. Each type of Omni Antenna is designed to radiate radio frequency signals in all directions, providing 360-degree coverage for wireless communication applications. These antennas are commonly used in WiFi routers, cellular base stations, and other wireless devices.</p></p>
