<p><p>Drilling mud additives are used in the oil and gas industry to enhance drilling performance. The market is divided into two main types: oil-based and water-based additives. Oil-based additives are effective in high-temperature environments and provide lubrication, while water-based additives are environmentally friendly and more cost-effective. Both types of additives improve drilling efficiency by controlling viscosity, reducing friction, and preventing formation damage. The choice between oil-based and water-based additives depends on the specific drilling conditions and environmental regulations.</p></p>
