<p><strong>The Welding Consumables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stick Electrodes</li><li>Solid Wires</li><li>Flux Cored Wires</li><li>SAW Wires & Fluxes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Welding consumables market consists of various types such as stick electrodes, solid wires, flux cored wires, SAW wires, and fluxes. Stick electrodes are widely used for manual welding processes, while solid wires are commonly used in MIG welding. Flux cored wires offer higher deposition rates and are suitable for welding thick materials. SAW wires are used in submerged arc welding processes for efficient and high-quality welds. Fluxes are used to protect the molten weld pool from atmospheric contamination during welding processes.</p></p>
