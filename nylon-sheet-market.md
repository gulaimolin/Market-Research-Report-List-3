<p><p>The Nylon Sheet market can be segmented into Nylon 6 Sheet and Nylon 66 Sheet. Nylon 66 Sheet, on the other hand, is made from polymerized hexamethylene diamine and adipic acid, offering similar properties but with higher melting point and stiffness. Other types of nylon sheets in the market may include blends of different nylon polymers or specialized formulations for specific applications.</p></p>
