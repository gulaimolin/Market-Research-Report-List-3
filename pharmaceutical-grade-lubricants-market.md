They are often preferred for their resistance to degradation and ability to maintain consistent viscosity. On the other hand, Non-Synthetic Lubricants are derived from natural sources like mineral oils and plant-based materials. While they may not offer the same level of performance as synthetic lubricants, they are often more cost-effective and environmentally friendly.</p></p>
