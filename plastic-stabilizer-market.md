<p><p>Plastic stabilizers are chemical additives used to enhance the stability of plastic materials during processing and end-use. The different types of plastic stabilizers include heat stabilizers, which prevent degradation at high temperatures; antioxidants, which protect against oxidation; light stabilizers, which prevent degradation from UV exposure; and others, which may include various additives such as lubricants and metal deactivators. Each type of stabilizer serves a specific purpose in improving the durability and performance of plastic products in different environmental conditions.</p></p>
