<p><p>DUV lithography systems are used in the semiconductor industry for manufacturing integrated circuits on silicon wafers. There are three main types of DUV lithography systems: ArF dry excimer systems, ArF immersion excimer systems, and KrF excimer systems. ArF dry excimer systems use a dry method for lithography, ArF immersion excimer systems use a liquid immersion method, and KrF excimer systems use a different wavelength of light for lithography. Each type of system has its own advantages and is used in different applications within the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
