<p><ul><li>Thermochromic</li><li>Photochromic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Specialty ink market types such as thermochromic and photochromic inks offer unique properties that change color in response to temperature or light respectively. Thermochromic inks are sensitive to heat, shifting in color when exposed to high temperatures and returning to their original color when cooled. On the other hand, photochromic inks react to ultraviolet light, causing a color change when exposed to sunlight and reverting back to their original color once removed from the light source. These specialty inks are used in various applications for decorative, security, and promotional purposes.</p></p>
