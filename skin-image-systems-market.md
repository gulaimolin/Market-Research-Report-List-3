<p><ul><li>Ultrasound Based Skin Imaging Systems</li><li>Optical Based Skin Imaging Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasound Based Skin Imaging Systems utilize sound waves to produce images of the skin layers, allowing for deep tissue visualization and analysis. These systems are widely used for assessing skin conditions such as cancer, psoriasis, and acne. In contrast, Optical Based Skin Imaging Systems use light to capture surface images of the skin, providing high-resolution images for analysis of pigmentation, texture, and other surface features. Both types of systems are essential in medical and cosmetic dermatology for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
