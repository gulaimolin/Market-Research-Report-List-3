<p><strong>The Overhead Railway Electrification System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DC Electrification System</li><li>AC Electrification System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Overhead Railway Electrification System market includes two main types: DC Electrification System and AC Electrification System. DC electrification system utilizes direct current to power the trains, providing a consistent and reliable source of electricity. On the other hand, AC electrification system uses alternating current, which is more commonly used due to its ability to be easily converted and transmitted over long distances. Both systems have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the railway network.</p></p>
