<p><p>IoT Smart Laser Sensors come in three main types: Diffuse Laser Sensor, Polarized Retroreflective Laser Sensor, and Through Beam Laser Sensor. Diffuse Laser Sensors detect objects based on the reflection of the laser beam from the object surface. Polarized Retroreflective Laser Sensors use a polarized laser beam to detect objects within a specific range. Through Beam Laser Sensors consist of two separate units - a transmitter and a receiver - which detect objects when the laser beam is interrupted. Each type of sensor has its own specific application in IoT smart technology.</p></p>
