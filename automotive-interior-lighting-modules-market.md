<p><p>Automotive Interior Lighting Modules consist of three main types - Halogen Lighting, HID Lighting, and LED Lighting. Halogen Lighting is the traditional choice, known for its warm and bright light. HID Lighting is more energy-efficient and has a longer lifespan compared to halogen, providing a crisp white light. LED Lighting is the newest technology, offering the highest energy efficiency, longer lifespan, and the ability to produce various colors. Each type caters to different preferences and needs of consumers in the automotive lighting market.</p></p>
