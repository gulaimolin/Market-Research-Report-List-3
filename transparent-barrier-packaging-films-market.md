The market types include Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP), known for its excellent barrier properties; Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC), preferred for its clarity and cost-effectiveness; Polylactic Acid (PLA), a biodegradable option; and Polyethylene (PE), offering good flexibility and toughness. Each type has its own unique properties and benefits for different packaging needs.</p></p>
