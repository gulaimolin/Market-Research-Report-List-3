<p><p>Floating dredging hoses come in two main types: flexible and hard (non-flexible). Flexible dredging hoses are designed to bend and move easily, allowing for greater mobility and versatility during dredging operations. They are typically made from rubber or other flexible materials that can withstand the harsh conditions of dredging applications. On the other hand, hard (non-flexible) dredging hoses are more rigid and less prone to bending. They are often made from materials like plastic or metal, providing increased durability and strength in challenging dredging environments.</p></p>
