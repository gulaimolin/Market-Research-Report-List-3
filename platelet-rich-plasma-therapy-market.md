<p><p>Platelet-rich Plasma Therapy market includes three main types: Pure PRP, Leucocyte-rich PRP, and Leukocyte-rich Fibrin (L-PRF). Pure PRP consists of high concentration of platelets, which release growth factors to promote tissue repair. Leucocyte-rich PRP contains white blood cells in addition to platelets, aiding in anti-inflammatory responses. Leukocyte-rich Fibrin (L-PRF) involves a natural fibrin matrix that traps platelets and leukocytes, enhancing healing properties. Each type offers specific advantages depending on the desired treatment outcomes.</p></p>
