<p><ul><li>Bullet Type</li><li>Dome Type</li><li>PTZ Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PoE security cameras are available in three main types: bullet type, dome type, and PTZ type. Bullet cameras are long and cylindrical in shape, making them ideal for outdoor use with a wide viewing angle. Dome cameras are compact and discreet, making them suitable for indoor environments. PTZ (pan-tilt-zoom) cameras offer the ability to remotely adjust the camera's position and zoom in on specific areas, providing flexibility and coverage for large areas. Each type caters to different security needs and preferences.</p></p>
