In the Direct Method, the graphitization process directly converts precursors into graphite, resulting in higher purity and better performance. On the other hand, the Indirect Method involves additional steps to convert precursors into graphitic carbon before graphitization. Each method has its own advantages and disadvantages, depending on factors such as cost, efficiency, and end-use application.</p></p>
