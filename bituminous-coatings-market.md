<p><p>Bituminous coatings market includes various types such as bitumen emulsion paint, rubberized bitumen emulsion, modified bitumen emulsion, and fibre reinforced solvented bitumen paint. Bitumen emulsion paint is a water-based coating used for waterproofing and corrosion protection. Rubberized bitumen emulsion is enhanced with rubber to improve flexibility and durability. Modified bitumen emulsion is a blend of bitumen and polymers for increased performance. Fibre reinforced solvented bitumen paint contains fibres for added strength and adhesion. These coatings cater to a wide range of applications in construction and infrastructure industries.</p></p>
