This approach allows operators to improve coverage and capacity while reducing interference and optimizing network performance.</p><p>Another trend shaping the market is the deployment of virtualized and cloud-based small cell solutions, which offer greater flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness compared to traditional hardware-based deployments.
