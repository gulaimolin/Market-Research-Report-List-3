<p><p>There are several types of radar markets including bistatic radar, continuous-wave radar, Doppler radar, FM-CW radar, monopulse radar, and others. Bistatic radar uses separate transmit and receive locations, continuous-wave radar emits a continuous signal, Doppler radar measures the velocity of an object, FM-CW radar uses frequency-modulated continuous waves, and monopulse radar improves accuracy by using multiple antennas. Other types of radar include synthetic aperture radar and phased array radar, each with unique features and applications in various industries.</p></p>
