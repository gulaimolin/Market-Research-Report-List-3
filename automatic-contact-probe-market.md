<p><ul><li>Infrared Transmission</li><li>Wireless Transmission</li><li>Hard-Wired Transmission</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for automatic contact probes can be classified into three main types based on the method of transmission: Infrared Transmission, Wireless Transmission, and Hard-Wired Transmission. Infrared transmission utilizes infrared signals to transfer data wirelessly between the probe and the receiving device. Wireless transmission involves the use of radio frequency signals to communicate without the need for physical cables. Hard-wired transmission uses physical cables to transfer data between the probe and the receiving device, ensuring a secure and reliable connection. Each type offers varying levels of convenience, flexibility, and reliability.</p></p>
