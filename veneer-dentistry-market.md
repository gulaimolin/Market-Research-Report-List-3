<p><ul><li>Composite Material</li><li>dental Porcelain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Veneer dentistry offers two main types of materials - composite and dental porcelain. Composite veneers are made from a tooth-colored resin material that is directly applied and molded to the tooth surface. They are a more cost-effective option and can be completed in a single visit. Dental porcelain veneers are custom-made shells that are bonded to the front of the teeth for a natural and long-lasting result. While porcelain veneers may require multiple visits and a higher cost, they are known for their durability and aesthetics.</p></p>
