<p><strong>The Thermal Tape Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-side</li><li>Double-side</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal tape is available in two main types: single-side and double-side. In the single-side market, the tape has adhesive on only one side, making it suitable for applications where only one surface needs to be bonded. On the other hand, double-side thermal tape has adhesive on both sides, allowing for bonding between two surfaces. Both types of thermal tape offer efficient heat conduction and are commonly used in various industries such as electronics, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
