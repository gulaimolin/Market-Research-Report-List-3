<p><p>Solder Preform is available in two main types: Lead Free and Leaded. Lead Free solder preforms are made without the use of lead, making them environmentally friendly and compliant with regulations. They are often used in industries where lead contamination is a concern. Leaded solder preforms, on the other hand, contain lead which helps improve the solder's flow and wetting properties. They are commonly used in applications where a higher level of performance is required. Both types of solder preforms offer different advantages depending on the specific needs of the application.</p></p>
