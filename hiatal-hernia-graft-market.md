<p><p>A Hiatal Hernia Graft market comprises two main types: Nonabsorbable Hernia Repair Mesh and Absorbable Hernia Repair Mesh. Nonabsorbable meshes are made of synthetic materials like polypropylene and are designed to provide long-term support to the herniated tissues. Absorbable meshes, on the other hand, gradually break down and are absorbed by the body over time. Both types of hernia repair meshes have their own advantages and are used based on the specific needs of the patient and the type of hernia being treated.</p></p>
