<p><p>Safety relays are devices used to control the flow of electricity in industrial machinery, ensuring safe operation. Timers are used to control the timing of various functions in machinery and automation systems.</p><p>Different types of safety relays include electromagnetic relays, thermal reed relays, and solid-state relays. Electromagnetic relays use an electromagnet to control the flow of electricity, thermal reed relays use thermally sensitive strips to open and close contacts, and solid-state relays use semiconductor devices to switch the current. Each type has its own advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
