<p><p>Low dropout regulators (LDOs) are voltage regulators that operate with a very small voltage difference between the input and output. They are designed to regulate voltage even when the input voltage is very close to the output voltage, making them ideal for applications where a small voltage drop is critical. LDOs are widely used in electronics for applications such as power management, battery-powered devices, and automotive systems.</p><p>The global Low Dropout Regulators Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.00% during the forecast period. Additionally, the expanding telecommunications and consumer electronics industries are also fueling the demand for LDOs.</p><p>The latest trends in the Low Dropout Regulators Market include the development of advanced LDO designs with improved efficiency, low noise, and smaller footprints.
