<p><p>Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) tags and labels come in three main types: hard, soft, and permanent deactivation. Hard tags are typically made of plastic or metal and require a special tool to be removed. Soft tags are made of paper or plastic and are usually disposable. Permanent deactivation tags are designed to be permanently deactivated at the point of sale. Each type offers varying levels of security, convenience, and cost effectiveness for retailers looking to protect their merchandise from theft.</p></p>
