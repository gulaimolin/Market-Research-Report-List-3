<p><p>Industrial checkweighers are used to ensure products are within specified weight limits. There are two main types in the market: in-motion checkweighers and intermittent checkweighers. In-motion checkweighers continuously weigh products as they move along a conveyor belt, allowing for high-speed operation in a production line. Intermittent checkweighers, on the other hand, weigh products in batches, making them suitable for slower production processes. Both types play a crucial role in quality control and compliance with regulations in various industries.</p></p>
