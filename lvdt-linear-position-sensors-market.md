<p><p>LVDT linear position sensors are available in two main market types - AC type and DC type. In the AC type market, sensors utilize an AC excitation signal to measure linear displacement. These sensors are known for their high accuracy and versatility in various applications. On the other hand, in the DC type market, sensors use a DC excitation signal to measure linear displacement. DC type sensors are preferred for their simplicity and reliability in industrial settings. Both market types cater to different requirements and offer unique advantages in terms of performance and usability.</p></p>
