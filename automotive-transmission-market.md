<p><p>Automotive transmission market types include Manual Transmission (MT), Automatic Transmission (AT), Automated Manual Transmission (AMT), Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT), and Dual Clutch Transmission (DCT). MT requires the driver to manually shift gears, while AT automatically changes gears. AMT combines the features of both MT and AT. CVT offers a smooth and continuous gear ratio without distinct gear shifts. DCT uses two clutches to shift gears faster than MT or AT. These different transmission types cater to varying driving preferences and performance needs in the automotive market.</p></p>
