<p><p>EPDM Rubber Compound Market Types - Suspension Polymerization and Solution Polymerization refer to different methods of producing EPDM rubber compounds. Suspension polymerization involves dispersing monomers in water and adding initiators to create polymer particles, while solution polymerization involves dissolving monomers in a solvent and adding initiators to create a solution of polymer chains. These methods result in different types of EPDM rubber compounds with varying properties and applications in industries such as automotive, construction, and electrical.</p></p>
