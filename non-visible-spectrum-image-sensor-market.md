<p><p>Linear image sensors are used for one-dimensional imaging in applications such as document scanning, barcode reading, and machine vision. They capture a single line of pixels at a time. Area image sensors, on the other hand, are used for two-dimensional imaging in cameras and video recorders. They capture an entire scene at once, resulting in higher quality images. Both types of sensors play important roles in various industries, with linear sensors suited for specific tasks requiring precise scanning and area sensors ideal for capturing detailed images.</p></p>
