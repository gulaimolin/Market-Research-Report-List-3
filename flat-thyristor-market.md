<p><p>Diode Thyristor is a type of flat thyristor that has two layers of alternating N and P type materials. Triode Thyristor has three layers of alternating N and P type materials, while Quadrupole Thyristor has four layers. The number of layers in each type affects their performance and capabilities. Diode Thyristors are the simplest and most basic, while Quadrupole Thyristors are more complex and efficient. Each type of flat thyristor has its own unique advantages and applications in the market.</p></p>
