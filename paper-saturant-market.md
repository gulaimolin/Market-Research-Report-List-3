<p><p>Paper saturants are commonly categorized into three main types: Vinyl Acetate/Ethylene (VAE), Vinyl Acrylic, and PVAc. VAE saturants are known for their high strength and flexibility, making them ideal for applications where durability is key. Vinyl Acrylic saturants offer improved water resistance and adhesion properties, perfect for applications that require resistance to moisture. PVAc saturants are valued for their excellent adhesion and low odor, making them suitable for a wide range of paper products. Each type offers unique qualities suited for different market needs.</p></p>
