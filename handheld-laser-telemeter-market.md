<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Handheld Laser Telemeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phase Method Telemeter</li><li>Pulse Method Telemeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld Laser Telemeters are available in two main types: Phase Method Telemeter and Pulse Method Telemeter. Phase Method Telemeters measure distance by analyzing the phase shift between the transmitted and received laser beams. They provide highly accurate measurements in various applications. Pulse Method Telemeters, on the other hand, determine distance by calculating the time it takes for the laser pulse to travel to the target and back. This type is suitable for measuring long distances and is commonly used in construction and surveying. Both types offer precise and reliable distance measurement capabilities for different industrial needs.</p></p>
