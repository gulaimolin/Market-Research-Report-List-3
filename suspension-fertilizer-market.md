<p><ul><li>Compound Suspension Fertilizer</li><li>Pure Inorganic Suspension Fertilizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Suspension fertilizers can be categorized into compound suspension fertilizers and pure inorganic suspension fertilizers. Compound suspension fertilizers contain a mix of different nutrients like nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium, as well as micronutrients, in a balanced ratio. On the other hand, pure inorganic suspension fertilizers contain only inorganic nutrients without any organic components. Both types of suspension fertilizers are used to provide essential nutrients to plants for their growth and development, but compound fertilizers offer a more comprehensive nutrient profile.</p></p>
