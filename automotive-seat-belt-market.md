<p><p>The Automotive Seat Belt market consists of two main types: ALR (Automatic Locking Retractor) Three Point Seatbelt and ELR (Emergency Locking Retractor) Three Point Seatbelt. ALR seatbelts automatically lock in place in the event of sudden braking or impact, providing added security and reducing the risk of injury. ELR seatbelts feature an emergency locking mechanism that engages during a collision, preventing passengers from being thrown forward. Both types of seatbelts are crucial safety features in vehicles today.</p></p>
