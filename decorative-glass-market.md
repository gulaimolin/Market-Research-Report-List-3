<p><p>The decorative glass market can be broadly categorized into three types: low-emissivity (Low-e) glass, special decorative glass, and other decorative glass products. Low-e glass is designed to minimize heat transfer and improve energy efficiency, making it ideal for windows in residential and commercial buildings. Special decorative glass includes unique designs, textures, and patterns that enhance aesthetics and provide creative options for interior and exterior decor. Other decorative glass products encompass a variety of customized and standard glass designs for different applications such as doors, partitions, and furniture.</p></p>
