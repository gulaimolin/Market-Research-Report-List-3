<p><p>Fire retardant gel market can be broadly categorized into two types: diluent and concentrate. On the other hand, concentrates are highly concentrated forms of gel that need to be diluted with water before application. Concentrates are usually preferred by industries and professionals who require large quantities of gel for fire protection.
