<p><p>Garlic Essence Market includes two main types: Dry Garlic Essence and Garlic Essence Oil market. Dry Garlic Essence is made by dehydrating fresh garlic to create a powder or granules, which can be easily stored and used as a flavoring agent in various dishes. On the other hand, Garlic Essence Oil market involves extracting the essential oils from garlic through a distillation process, resulting in a concentrated liquid form that is commonly used for cooking, aromatherapy, and medicinal purposes. Both types offer unique flavor profiles and health benefits.</p></p>
