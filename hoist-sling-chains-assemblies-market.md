<p><p>Hoist sling chains assemblies are commonly available in two main types: single leg and double leg. Single leg assemblies consist of a single chain with attachment points at each end, making them suitable for lifting loads in a straight line. On the other hand, double leg assemblies feature two chains connected to a master link, offering enhanced stability and weight distribution for more complex lifting applications. Both types are widely used in various industries for their durability and versatility in handling heavy loads.</p></p>
