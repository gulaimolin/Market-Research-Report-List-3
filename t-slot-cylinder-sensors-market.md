<p><p>T slot cylinder sensors are used to detect the position of cylinders in automated machinery. PNP (positive-negative-positive) cylinder sensors use a positive voltage to signal the presence of the cylinder, while NPN (negative-positive-negative) sensors use a negative voltage. PNP sensors are often used in applications where the output signal needs to be high, while NPN sensors are suitable for applications where the output needs to be low. Both types are crucial for ensuring proper functioning of automated systems.</p></p>
