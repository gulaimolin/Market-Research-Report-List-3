<p><ul><li>Low Carbon Steel</li><li>High Carbon Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carbon Steel Round Bar Market can be classified into Low Carbon Steel and High Carbon Steel. Low carbon steel round bars have a carbon content of less than 0.3%, making them more ductile and easy to weld. They are commonly used in construction, automotive, and manufacturing industries. On the other hand, High carbon steel round bars have a carbon content of 0.6% to 1.5%, which makes them harder and stronger. They are used in applications that require high strength and durability, such as in tools, machinery, and equipment manufacturing.</p></p>
