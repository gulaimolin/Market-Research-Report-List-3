<p><p>The mechanical trap market consists of various types such as Ball Float Type, Semi Ball Float Type, Lever Float Type, Inverted Bucket Type. The Ball Float Type trap uses a ball float to control the opening and closing of the valve. The Semi Ball Float Type trap operates similarly but with a smaller float. The Lever Float Type trap utilizes a lever mechanism to regulate the valve. The Inverted Bucket Type trap uses an inverted bucket to automatically open and close the valve. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
