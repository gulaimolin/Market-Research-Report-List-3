<p><p>Car paint protective film is a type of clear protective layer applied to the surface of vehicles to protect them from scratches, rock chips, and other damages. There are two main types of materials used in the market: TPU (thermoplastic polyurethane) and PE (polyethylene). TPU films are known for their high durability, flexibility, and self-healing properties. On the other hand, PE films are cost-effective and offer good protection against minor damages. Both types of films provide varying levels of protection and are popular choices among car owners.</p></p>
