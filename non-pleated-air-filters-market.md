<p><p>Non-pleated air filters are categorized based on their Minimum Efficiency Reporting Value (MERV) ratings. The MERV 3 and Below market includes basic filters that provide minimal filtration efficiency. Filters with MERV ratings of 4 to 7 offer moderate efficiency in capturing particles. On the other hand, filters with MERV 8 and Above ratings are considered high-efficiency filters that can effectively trap smaller particles. Consumers can choose the appropriate filter based on their specific air quality needs and requirements.</p></p>
