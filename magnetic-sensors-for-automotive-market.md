<p><ul><li>Linear Magnetic Sensor</li><li>Angular Magnetic Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Linear magnetic sensors for automotive measure linear displacement or position using the principle of magnetic induction. They are commonly used in applications such as throttle position sensing and gearbox position detection. Angular magnetic sensors, on the other hand, measure angular displacement or rotation using magnetic fields. They are widely utilized in automotive applications such as steering angle sensing and pedal position detection. Both types of magnetic sensors play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and efficiency of automotive systems.</p></p>
