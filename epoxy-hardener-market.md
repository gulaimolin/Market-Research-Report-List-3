<p><p>Epoxy hardeners are categorized into three main types: amine-based products, anhydride-based products, and other types. Amine-based hardeners are known for their fast curing times and high performance in various applications. Anhydride-based hardeners are chosen for their excellent chemical and temperature resistance. Other types of epoxy hardeners may include phenol-based hardeners, dicyandiamide hardeners, and mercaptan-based hardeners, each offering unique characteristics and advantages depending on the specific application requirements. These three main types of epoxy hardeners cater to a wide range of industries and applications.</p></p>
