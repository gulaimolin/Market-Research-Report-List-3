<p><p>The Generator Circuit Breaker (GCB) market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. Plug-in GCB: These are portable and can be easily connected and disconnected from the generator circuit. They are suitable for temporary power solutions.</p><p>2. Fixed GCB: These are permanently mounted to the generator and cannot be easily removed. They are typically used in stationary power generation applications.</p><p>3. Drawer GCB: These are designed to be easily inserted and removed from the generator without disconnecting any wiring. They offer flexibility and ease of maintenance.</p></p>
