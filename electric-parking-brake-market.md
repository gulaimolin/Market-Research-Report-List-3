<p><strong>The Electric Parking Brake Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Caliper Integrated EPB</li><li>Cable Puller EPB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric parking brakes (EPB) come in two main types: caliper integrated EPB and cable puller EPB. Caliper integrated EPB is a system where the parking brake functionality is integrated within the brake caliper itself. On the other hand, cable puller EPB uses a system of cables to engage the parking brake when needed. Both types offer advantages in terms of space-saving design, improved safety, and ease of use compared to traditional manual parking brake systems, making them popular choices in the automotive market.</p></p>
