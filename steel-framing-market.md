The Modular Structural Frame entails pre-fabricated steel components that are assembled on-site, providing a cost-effective and efficient building solution. Single Slope Frame Style features a roof with a single slope, ideal for buildings requiring a modern aesthetic or to accommodate drainage needs. These different types cater to varying construction needs and offer distinct advantages in terms of design flexibility and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
