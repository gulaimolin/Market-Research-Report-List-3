There are different types of hydraulic seals in the market, including hydraulic piston seals which are used to seal the reciprocating motion of hydraulic cylinders, hydraulic rod seals which prevent fluid leakage from the cylinder's pressure side, and hydraulic wiper seals which protect the system from contaminants. Each type of seal plays a crucial role in ensuring the efficient and reliable operation of hydraulic systems.</p></p>
