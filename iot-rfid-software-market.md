<p><strong>The IoT RFID Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive RFID</li><li>Active RFID</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passive RFID software market involves tags that do not have a power source and rely on the reader's signal for data transmission. This type is cost-effective but has limited range and read speed. Active RFID software market uses tags with a power source, enabling longer read ranges and faster data transmission. Though more expensive, active RFID is ideal for tracking high-value assets or monitoring large areas. Both types play a crucial role in IoT applications, providing real-time data and insights for various industries.</p></p>
