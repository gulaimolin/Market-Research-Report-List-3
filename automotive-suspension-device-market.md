<p><p>The automotive suspension device market consists of various types including Leaf Spring, Spiral Spring, Hydro-pneumatic Spring, Torsion Bar Spring, and others. Leaf springs are made up of multiple layers of metal strips stacked together. Spiral springs use coils to absorb and distribute shock. Hydro-pneumatic springs use a combination of gas and liquid to absorb shocks. Torsion bar springs use a metal bar to provide resistance to twisting forces. Other types may include air suspension systems, rubber bushings, or magnetic suspension systems.</p></p>
