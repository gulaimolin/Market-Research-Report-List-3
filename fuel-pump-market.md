<p><strong>The Fuel Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Common Fuel Pump</li><li>GDI Fuel Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Common Fuel Pump: The common fuel pump is a traditional type of fuel pump that is used in most vehicles with conventional engines. It delivers fuel from the tank to the engine at a consistent rate, ensuring proper combustion and engine performance.</p><p>GDI Fuel Pump: Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI) fuel pumps are specifically designed for use in vehicles with direct injection engines. These pumps deliver fuel at a high pressure directly into the combustion chamber, resulting in improved fuel efficiency and power output. GDI fuel pumps are more technologically advanced than common fuel pumps and are becoming increasingly common in modern vehicles.</p></p>
