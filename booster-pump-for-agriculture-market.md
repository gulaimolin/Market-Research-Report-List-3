<p><p>Booster pumps for agriculture are available in two main types - single stage and multiple stage. Single stage pumps are designed to boost water pressure in a single operation, making them more suitable for smaller scale applications. Multiple stage pumps, on the other hand, use multiple impellers to gradually increase water pressure, making them ideal for larger scale agricultural operations where higher pressures are required. Both types of booster pumps are essential for maintaining efficient irrigation systems in agriculture.</p></p>
