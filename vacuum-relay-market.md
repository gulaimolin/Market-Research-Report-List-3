<p><strong>The Vacuum Relay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DPDT</li><li>SPDT</li><li>SPST</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vacuum relays come in different types, such as Double Pole Double Throw (DPDT), Single Pole Double Throw (SPDT), and Single Pole Single Throw (SPST). DPDT relays have two input/output circuits and can switch between two different output paths. SPDT relays have one input and two output paths, allowing for a choice between two different outputs. SPST relays have one input and one output path, providing a simple on/off function. Each type of vacuum relay offers different capabilities and applications based on the specific needs of the user.</p></p>
