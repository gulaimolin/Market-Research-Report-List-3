<p><p>Electric travel mobility scooters are categorized into Class 2 and Class 3 scooters based on their design and specifications. Class 2 scooters are designed for pavement use only, with a maximum speed of 4mph. They are more compact and lightweight, making them suitable for indoor use as well. On the other hand, Class 3 scooters can reach speeds of up to 8mph and are equipped with lights and indicators for road use. They are typically larger and more robust, offering a higher level of comfort and stability for longer journeys.</p></p>
