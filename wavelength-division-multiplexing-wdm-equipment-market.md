<p><ul><li>CWDM Equipment</li><li>DWDM Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wavelength Division Multiplexing (WDM) Equipment Market includes two main types: Coarse Wavelength Division Multiplexing (CWDM) Equipment and Dense Wavelength Division Multiplexing (DWDM) Equipment. CWDM Equipment is able to multiplex up to 18 different streams of data onto a single fiber optic cable, while DWDM Equipment can multiplex up to 80 or more streams. CWDM is typically used for shorter distances and lower data rates, while DWDM is preferred for longer distances and higher data rates due to its ability to support a greater number of wavelengths.</p></p>
