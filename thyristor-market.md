<p><p>Thyristors are semiconductor devices used for controlling electrical power in various applications. Unidirectional Thyristors conduct current only in one direction and are commonly used in rectifier circuits. On the other hand, Bidirectional Thyristors can conduct current in both directions and are frequently used in applications requiring controlled AC power. Both types of Thyristors are essential in industries such as power electronics, telecommunications, and automotive, contributing to the growth of the global Thyristor market.</p></p>
