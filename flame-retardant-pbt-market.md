<p><strong>The Flame Retardant PBT Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Halogen Flame Retardant</li><li>Halogen-free Flame Retardant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flame retardant PBT market types can be categorized into two main categories: Halogen Flame Retardant and Halogen-free Flame Retardant. </p><p>Halogen Flame Retardant PBT compounds contain halogen elements such as bromine or chlorine, which are effective in slowing down the spread of fire. However, concerns have been raised about the environmental and health impacts of halogenated flame retardants.</p><p>On the other hand, Halogen-free Flame Retardant PBT compounds use alternative materials such as phosphorus, nitrogen, or mineral fillers to achieve flame retardant properties without the use of halogen elements. This type of flame retardant is considered to be more environmentally friendly and safer for human health.</p></p>
