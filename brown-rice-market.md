<p><p>Brown rice is classified into different market types based on the size and shape of the grain. Short grain brown rice is plump and sticky, often used in sushi and risotto. Medium grain brown rice is slightly less sticky and commonly used in dishes like paella and pilaf. Long grain brown rice is slender and firm, perfect for dishes like stir-fries and salads. Other market types of brown rice include aromatic varieties like jasmine and basmati, each with their unique flavor profiles and culinary uses.</p></p>
