<p><p>Cardiac stress test equipment market includes two main types: equipment with treadmill and equipment with exercise bike. Both types are utilized to measure the heart's response to physical exertion. The equipment with treadmill involves walking or running on a moving platform while heart rate and other vital signs are monitored. On the other hand, equipment with exercise bike simulates cycling to test cardiac function. These tests help in diagnosing coronary artery disease, evaluating exercise capacity, and monitoring the heart's response to stress.</p></p>
