<p><p>Oral cavity collagen membranes, made from either cowhide or pigskin, are used in dental procedures to aid in tissue regeneration and wound healing. Cowhide membranes are known for their durability and strength, making them suitable for guided tissue regeneration. Pigskin membranes, on the other hand, are more flexible and conform easily to the contour of the oral cavity. Both types of collagen membranes offer biocompatibility, support soft tissue healing, and prevent unwanted tissue invasion during the healing process in dental surgeries.</p></p>
