<p><ul><li>General Heat Treatment</li><li>Surface Heat Treatment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heat treating services can be divided into two main types: general heat treatment and surface heat treatment. General heat treatment involves altering the properties of an entire component through processes like annealing, normalizing, or quenching. This type of treatment is typically used to improve the overall strength and hardness of a material. On the other hand, surface heat treatment focuses on modifying only the surface of a component through methods like carburizing, nitriding, or induction hardening. This type of treatment is often used to enhance the wear resistance and durability of a material's surface.</p></p>
