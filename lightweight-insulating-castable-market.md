<p><strong>The Lightweight Insulating Castable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Casting Type</li><li>Dry Gunning Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lightweight insulating castable market consists of two main types: casting type and dry gunning type. Casting type involves pouring or pumping the castable material into molds to create insulating shapes. This type is preferred for creating custom shapes or linings in various industrial applications. On the other hand, dry gunning type involves spraying the castable material onto a surface using a compressed air gun. This type is commonly used for repairing or lining existing structures that require insulation.</p></p>
