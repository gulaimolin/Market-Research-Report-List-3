<p><ul><li>Cured-On Solid Tire</li><li>Pressed-On Solid Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The forklift solid tire market is segmented into two main types: Cured-On Solid Tire and Pressed-On Solid Tire. Cured-On solid tires are manufactured by curing rubber onto a metal ring, providing enhanced durability and a longer lifespan. Pressed-On solid tires are constructed by pressing a rubber tire onto a metal wheel, offering a cost-effective solution for forklift operators. Both types provide improved traction, stability, and longevity compared to traditional pneumatic tires, making them popular choices for industrial use.</p></p>
