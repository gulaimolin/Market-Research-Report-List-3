<p><p>Disposable Nitrile Examination Gloves come in two primary types: Powdered and Powder-Free. Powdered gloves are lubricated with cornstarch to make them easier to put on and remove, but this powder can cause allergies in some users and contaminate certain environments. On the other hand, Powder-Free gloves are free from cornstarch and are thus safer for those with allergies and for use in sensitive areas. Both types are widely used in healthcare settings, laboratories, food service, and other industries requiring hand protection.</p></p>
