<p><strong>The SB Latex Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emulsion Styrene Butadiene Latex</li><li>Solution Styrene Butadiene Latex</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SB latex market can be divided into two types: Emulsion Styrene Butadiene Latex and Solution Styrene Butadiene Latex. Emulsion SB latex is a water-based dispersion of synthetic polymers, commonly used in adhesives, coatings, and paper coatings due to its excellent adhesive properties. On the other hand, Solution SB latex is a solvent-based solution of the same polymers, often utilized in tire manufacturing and carpet backing. Both types offer unique advantages and are preferred in different industries based on their specific properties and applications.</p></p>
