<p><p>Zwitterionic surfactants market consists of various types such as betaine, amine oxide, amphoacetates, amphopropionates, and sultaines. Betaine surfactants are derived from amino acids and are known for their mildness and conditioning properties. Amine oxide surfactants are effective foam boosters and stabilizers. Amphoacetates and amphopropionates are amphoteric surfactants with good compatibility and low irritation potential. Sultaines are versatile surfactants with excellent compatibility and foaming properties. Each type offers specific benefits and applications in the surfactants market.</p></p>
