<p><strong>The Dental Ultrasonic Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetostrictive</li><li>Piezoelectric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental Ultrasonic Device Market consists of two main types: magnetostrictive and piezoelectric. Magnetostrictive devices use a magnetic field to vibrate a metal stack, creating high-frequency vibrations for efficient removal of plaque and tartar. On the other hand, piezoelectric devices use crystals that expand and contract when an electrical current is applied, generating ultrasonic vibrations. Both technologies offer precise and effective cleaning during dental procedures. Each type has its unique strengths and features, catering to the diverse needs of dental professionals.</p></p>
