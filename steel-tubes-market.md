<p><ul><li>Welded Tube</li><li>Seamless Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steel tubes market can be classified into two main types: welded tubes and seamless tubes. Welded tubes are made by rolling a flat strip of steel into a cylinder and welding the edges together, while seamless tubes are made by piercing a solid steel billet to create a hollow tube. Welded tubes are more cost-effective and commonly used in construction and infrastructure projects, while seamless tubes are preferred for applications requiring high pressure or temperature resistance, such as in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
