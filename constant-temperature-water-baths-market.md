<p><ul><li>Circulating Water Baths</li><li>Non-Circulating Water Baths</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Constant temperature water baths are widely used in various industries and laboratories for maintaining a stable temperature for samples. Circulating water baths utilize a pump to circulate water around the bath for more uniform temperature distribution. Non-circulating water baths rely on natural convection to achieve temperature stability. Both types offer precise temperature control for a wide range of applications, with circulating baths being more suitable for tasks requiring rapid temperature changes and non-circulating baths being more cost-effective for simpler applications.</p></p>
