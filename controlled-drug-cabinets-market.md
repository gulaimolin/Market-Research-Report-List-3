<p><p>Controlled drug cabinets come in four main types: Tall drug cabinets are designed for storing a large quantity of medications and substances. Wall drug cabinets are mounted on walls to save space and provide easy access. Base drug cabinets are installed on the ground and are suitable for storing heavy items.
