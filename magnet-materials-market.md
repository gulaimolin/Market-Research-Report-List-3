<p><p>Permanent magnetic materials are used in applications where a strong and constant magnetic field is required, such as in electric motors and hard disk drives. Soft magnetic materials, on the other hand, are used in applications where the magnetic field needs to be easily changed or manipulated, such as in transformers and inductors. The market for permanent magnetic materials is driven by the growing demand for consumer electronics and renewable energy technologies, while the market for soft magnetic materials is driven by the automotive and industrial sectors.</p></p>
