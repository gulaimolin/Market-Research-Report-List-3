<p><strong>The Photovoltaic Metallization Paste Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>BSF Solar Cells</li><li>PERC Solar Cells</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photovoltaic Metallization Paste is used in both BSF (Back Surface Field) solar cells and PERC (Passivated Emitter and Rear Cell) solar cells. In BSF solar cells, the metallization paste is applied to the back surface of the solar cell to improve conductivity and efficiency. In PERC solar cells, the paste is used on both the front and rear surfaces to enhance light absorption and electron extraction. These different market types cater to the specific needs and requirements of each type of solar cell technology.</p></p>
