<p><p>Metal laser cutting solutions are classified into different types based on the method used in the cutting process. Laser vaporization cutting involves using a high-power laser beam to vaporize the material, while laser melting cutting uses a focused laser beam to melt and remove the material. Laser oxygen cutting utilizes a laser beam combined with oxygen to achieve a rapid oxidation process for cutting. Other types of metal laser cutting solutions may include laser ablation cutting, laser scribing cutting, and laser drilling cutting, each offering unique advantages in specific applications.</p></p>
