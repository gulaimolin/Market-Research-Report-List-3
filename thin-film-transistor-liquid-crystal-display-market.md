<p><p>Thin-film transistor-liquid crystal display (TFT-LCD) market is classified into three main types: TN (Twisted Nematic), VA (Vertical Alignment), and IPS (In-Plane Switching). TN panels are commonly used due to their fast response times and low production costs. VA panels offer better color reproduction and viewing angles compared to TN panels. IPS panels provide the best color accuracy and viewing angles among the three types. Each market type caters to different consumer needs and preferences in terms of visual quality and performance.</p></p>
