<p><p>The automotive wiper components market consists of three main types - wiper blades, wiper motors, and rain sensors. Wiper blades are the rubber parts that come into direct contact with the windshield to clear water and debris. Wiper motors are responsible for powering the movement of the wiper blades. Rain sensors are electronic devices that automatically detect moisture on the windshield and trigger the wiper system to activate. These components work together to ensure clear visibility for drivers in various weather conditions.</p></p>
