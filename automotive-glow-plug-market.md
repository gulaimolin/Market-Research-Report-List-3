<p><p>The automotive glow plug market is classified into two main types: metal glow plugs and ceramic glow plugs. Metal glow plugs are typically made of metal alloy materials and are known for their durability and longevity. They are suitable for diesel engines that require high temperatures for combustion. On the other hand, ceramic glow plugs are made of ceramic materials and are known for their quick heating capabilities and efficient fuel consumption. They are commonly used in modern diesel engines for improved performance and reduced emissions.</p></p>
