<p><p>Medium Density Fiberboard (MDF) is available in three main types in the market. Fire-rated MDF is specifically designed to resist fire and prevent its spread, making it suitable for use in buildings with strict fire safety regulations. Moisture Resistant MDF is treated to repel moisture, making it ideal for use in areas with high humidity levels, such as kitchens and bathrooms. General MDF is the most common type and is used for a wide range of applications due to its versatility and affordability.</p></p>
