<p><ul><li>Propeller Shaft</li><li>Half Shaft</li><li>Axle Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Axle shaft market can be categorized into three main types: Propeller shaft, Half shaft, and Axle shaft. Propeller shafts are used in vehicles with rear-wheel or all-wheel drive, transferring power from the transmission to the rear axle. Half shafts connect the differential to the drive wheels in front-wheel drive vehicles. Axle shafts transmit power from the differential to the drive wheels in rear-wheel drive vehicles. Each type of shaft plays a crucial role in transferring power and torque to the wheels, allowing for smooth and efficient vehicle operation.</p></p>
