<p><p>Composites are materials made up of two or more components with different properties, designed to work together to create a stronger, lighter and more durable product. In the automotive market, there are three main types of composites: Polymer Matrix Composites (PMC), Metal Matrix Composites (MMC), and Ceramic Matrix Composites (CMC). PMC are made of polymers reinforced with fibers, MMC consist of metal matrices with ceramic reinforcement, and CMC comprise of ceramic matrices reinforced with ceramic fibers. These composites are utilized in various automotive components to improve performance and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
