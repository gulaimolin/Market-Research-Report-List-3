<p><strong>The Small Cell Networks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Femtocell</li><li>Picocell</li><li>Microcell</li><li>Metrocell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Small cell networks are divided into various market types based on their coverage and capacity. Femtocells are designed for residential or small business use, providing indoor coverage. Picocells cover larger indoor spaces or outdoor areas like stadiums or shopping malls. Microcells have a larger coverage area compared to picocells and are used in urban or suburban areas. Metrocells are the largest of the small cell types, providing coverage in dense urban areas and acting as an extension of the macrocell network.</p></p>
