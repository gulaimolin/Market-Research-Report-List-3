<p><p>Microbolometer infrared detectors are commonly used in thermal imaging technologies and are available in different types based on the material used for the sensing element. The most common types are Vanadium Oxide (VOx) and Amorphous Silicon (A-Si). VOx detectors offer high sensitivity and performance, while A-Si detectors are cost-effective and robust. Other types of microbolometer detectors include materials like Germanium or Platinum, each offering unique benefits such as higher resolution or better temperature stability depending on the application requirements.</p></p>
