<p><ul><li>Magnetostrictive</li><li>Piezoelectric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasonic jewelry cleaner machines come in two main types: magnetostrictive and piezoelectric. Magnetostrictive machines use a coil to generate sound waves and clean jewelry by creating vibration in the cleaning solution. Piezoelectric machines, on the other hand, use a ceramic disc to convert electrical energy into ultrasonic waves, providing a gentler cleaning process for delicate jewelry pieces. Both types of machines are popular in the market and offer effective solutions for keeping jewelry clean and shiny.</p></p>
