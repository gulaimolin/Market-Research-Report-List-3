<p><p>DC Linear Variable Displacement Transformers (LVDT) market includes different types such as Free Core, Guided Core, Sprung Core, and Others. Free Core LVDTs have a movable core that slides freely within the coil assembly. Guided Core LVDTs have a core that is attached to a shaft, providing stability and guidance. Sprung Core LVDTs have a pre-loaded spring to maintain the core in a neutral position. Other types of LVDTs may include variations or combinations of these designs to suit specific applications and requirements.</p></p>
