<p><ul><li>Analog</li><li>Digital</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Media gateways come in two main types: Analog and Digital. Analog media gateways typically convert traditional analog signals to digital signals for transmission over IP networks. They are commonly used in legacy phone systems to connect to modern VoIP networks. On the other hand, Digital media gateways are designed to handle digital signals directly, without the need for conversion. They are typically used in modern telecommunications systems to connect various types of digital devices and networks, providing a seamless communication experience.</p></p>
