<p><p>Synthetic adsorbents can be categorized into different types based on their composition and structure. Polystyrenic type adsorbents are made from cross-linked polystyrene polymers which provide high surface area and mechanical stability. Methacrylic type adsorbents are made from methacrylate polymers which offer excellent chemical and thermal stability. Both types of adsorbents are commonly used in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and environmental remediation for the removal of impurities and contaminants from liquids and gases.</p></p>
