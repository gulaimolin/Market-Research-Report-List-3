<p><p>IoT Smart Proximity Sensors are categorized into different types based on their technology and applications. Capacitive Proximity Sensors detect objects based on changes in capacitance, making them suitable for non-metallic objects. Inductive Proximity Sensors use electromagnetic fields to detect metal objects without physical contact. Photoelectric Proximity Sensors use light beams to sense objects, making them ideal for longer distances. Other types of Proximity Sensors include ultrasonic sensors and magnetic proximity sensors, each offering unique features for specific applications in IoT devices.</p></p>
