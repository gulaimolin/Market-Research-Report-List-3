<p><p>Smartphone screens come in various types such as LCD, OLED, AMOLED, and others. LCD (liquid crystal display) screens use a backlight to illuminate the pixels, while OLED (organic light-emitting diode) screens emit light directly from each pixel, resulting in more vibrant colors and deeper blacks. AMOLED (active-matrix OLED) screens are a type of OLED display with improved power efficiency and faster response times. Other screens in the market include technologies like e-ink and TFT LCD, each with their own advantages and limitations.</p></p>
