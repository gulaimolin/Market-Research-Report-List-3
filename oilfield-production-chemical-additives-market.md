Gas hydrate inhibitors prevent the formation of gas hydrates. Demulsifiers are used to separate water from oil. Lubricants are used to reduce friction. Biocides are used to control bacterial growth. Corrosion inhibitors protect equipment from corrosion. Dispersants and cleaning agents remove unwanted substances. H2S scavengers remove hydrogen sulfide gas.</p></p>
