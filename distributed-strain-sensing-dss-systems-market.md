<p><p>Distributed Strain Sensing (DSS) Systems utilize fiber optic technology to measure strain and temperature along the entire length of the fiber. There are two main market types for DSS systems: Single Mode Fiber-Based DSS and Multimode Fiber-Based DSS. Single Mode Fiber-Based DSS systems offer high resolution and accuracy over long distances, making them suitable for structural health monitoring applications. On the other hand, Multimode Fiber-Based DSS systems offer lower cost and easier installation, making them ideal for shorter-range applications such as monitoring pipelines and tunnels.</p></p>
