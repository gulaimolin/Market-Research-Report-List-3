<p><p>Optics Modules Market includes single mode and multi mode optical modules. Single mode optical modules are designed for long-distance transmission with a single beam of light, offering higher data rates and lower signal loss. Multi mode optical modules are used for shorter distances and offer lower data rates compared to single mode modules. Both types cater to different needs in telecommunications, networking, and data centers. Single mode modules are preferred for long-haul applications, while multi mode modules are suitable for connecting devices within the same location.</p></p>
