<p><p>The polymer flexible hose market consists of three main types: thermoset elastomers, thermoplastic resins, and thermoplastic elastomers. Thermoset elastomers are rubber-like materials that can regain their original shape after being stretched. Thermoplastic resins are solid materials that can be melted and reformed multiple times. Thermoplastic elastomers combine the flexibility of elastomers with the processability of thermoplastics. Each type of material offers unique properties that cater to different applications within the flexible hose market.</p></p>
