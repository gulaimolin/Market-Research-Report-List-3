<p><p>Interview Microphones are essential tools for capturing high-quality audio during interviews. Lavalier Microphones are small and discreet, commonly clipped onto the speaker's clothing for hands-free use. They are ideal for capturing clear audio in a controlled environment. On the other hand, Shotgun Microphones have a more directional pickup pattern, ideal for capturing sound from a specific direction. They are commonly used in noisy environments or outdoor settings to isolate the speaker's voice from ambient noise. Both types cater to different needs in the market.</p></p>
