<p><p>Enteral Nutrition (EN) Solutions come in various types to provide nourishment for individuals who are unable to eat orally. Naso Gastric (NG) Feeding involves delivering the solution into the stomach through a tube inserted into the nose. Naso Duodenal Feeding targets the duodenum for nutrient absorption. Naso Jejunal (NJ) Feeding delivers the solution to the jejunum, bypassing the stomach. Other types of EN solutions cater to specific needs and conditions of patients, providing alternative methods for delivering essential nutrients.</p></p>
