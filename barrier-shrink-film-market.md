<p><p>Barrier shrink film can be classified into four market types based on their barrier properties: Low, Medium, High, and Ultra-high. Low barrier films offer basic protection against oxygen and moisture, suitable for short shelf-life products. Medium barrier films provide moderate protection, ideal for products with a longer shelf life. High barrier films offer superior protection against oxygen and moisture, extending the shelf life of sensitive products. Ultra-high barrier films provide the highest level of protection, ensuring the longest possible shelf life for highly perishable products.</p></p>
