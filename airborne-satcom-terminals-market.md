<p><p>Airborne SATCOM terminals in Ka-band and Ku-band are two market types for providing satellite communication services to aircraft. Ka-band terminals operate at higher frequencies, offering faster data rates and greater bandwidth for high-speed internet access and video streaming. On the other hand, Ku-band terminals operate at lower frequencies, providing reliable connectivity for voice communications and basic data transfer. Both market types cater to different requirements and preferences of airborne users, ranging from commercial airlines to military aircraft.</p></p>
