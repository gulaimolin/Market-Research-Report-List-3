<p><p>The harmonica market is divided into several types including diatonic harmonicas, chromatic harmonicas, tremolo harmonicas, and other types. Diatonic harmonicas are known for their simplicity and are often used in blues, folk, and country music. Chromatic harmonicas have a larger range and are capable of playing all 12 notes in the Western scale. Tremolo harmonicas produce a wavering sound due to their double holes. Other types of harmonicas include octave, bass, and orchestral harmonicas, each with their own unique characteristics and uses.</p></p>
