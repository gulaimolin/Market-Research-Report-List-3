<p><p>RF Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) antennas are classified into two main types based on their configuration: Single Type and Pair Type. Single Type antennas consist of a single unit that emits radio frequency waves to detect tagged items passing through it. Pair Type antennas, on the other hand, are composed of two units placed facing each other to create a detection field between them. Each type has its own advantages and applications, catering to different security needs in retail stores and other industries.</p></p>
