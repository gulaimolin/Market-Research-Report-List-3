<p><strong>The Truck Alloy Wheel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Casting</li><li>Forging</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Truck alloy wheels are typically categorized into three types based on the manufacturing process: casting, forging, and others. Casting involves pouring molten metal into a mold to create the wheel, resulting in a cost-effective option with good design flexibility. Forging involves shaping the metal while it is still hot, leading to stronger and more durable wheels but at a higher cost. The "other" category may include hybrid wheels or those made from alternative manufacturing processes. Each type offers unique benefits and characteristics for truck owners to consider.</p></p>
