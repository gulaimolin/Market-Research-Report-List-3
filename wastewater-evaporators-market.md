<p><p>Wastewater evaporators are used for treating wastewater by evaporating liquid waste and leaving behind solids. There are different types of wastewater evaporators, including floating evaporators and trajectory evaporators. Floating evaporators are designed to float on the surface of a body of water and evaporate liquid waste. Trajectory evaporators use a system of nozzles to spray wastewater into the air, allowing it to evaporate before settling back down. These technologies are used in various industries for wastewater treatment.</p></p>
