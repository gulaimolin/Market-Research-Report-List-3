<p><p>The 5G mmWave Antenna Module market comprises Phased Array Antenna Modules and RF Transceiver Antenna Modules. Phased Array Antenna Modules use multiple antennas to create a beam of radio waves that can be steered electronically, allowing for faster and more precise communication. RF Transceiver Antenna Modules integrate both the radio frequency transmitter and receiver into a single module, enabling efficient transmission and reception of signals. Both types play a crucial role in enabling high-speed data transfer and low latency in 5G networks.</p></p>
