<p><p>Laboratory refrigerated centrifuges are commonly available in two types: desktop and floor-standing. Desktop centrifuges are compact and convenient for small laboratories or when space is limited. They are designed to sit on a benchtop and typically have lower capacity than floor-standing models. Floor-standing centrifuges are larger and offer higher capacity, making them suitable for larger laboratories with higher sample volumes. They are often equipped with more advanced features and capabilities, such as refrigeration. Both types of centrifuges are essential for various applications in biological and chemical research.</p></p>
