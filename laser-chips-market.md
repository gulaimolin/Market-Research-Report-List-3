<p><p>Laser chips are semiconductor devices that emit coherent light through the process of stimulated emission. Two main types of laser chips are VCSEL laser chips and semiconductor laser chips. VCSEL laser chips are used in applications such as optical communications, sensing, and 3D sensing technology. Meanwhile, semiconductor laser chips are widely used in a variety of industries including healthcare, military, telecommunications, and industrial manufacturing. Both types of laser chips are essential components in modern technology and continue to drive innovation in various sectors.</p></p>
