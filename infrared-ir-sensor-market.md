<p><ul><li>Thermal Infrared (TIR) Sensor</li><li>Quantum Infrared (QIR) Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Infrared IR sensors can be divided into two main types: Thermal Infrared (TIR) Sensors and Quantum Infrared (QIR) Sensors. TIR sensors detect infrared radiation emitted by objects based on their temperature, making them suitable for applications such as temperature measurement and thermal imaging. On the other hand, QIR sensors utilize quantum mechanics principles to detect infrared radiation, offering higher sensitivity and precision for applications like spectroscopy and quantum communication. Both types of sensors play important roles in various industries such as healthcare, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
