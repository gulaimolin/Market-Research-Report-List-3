<p><p>Fiber to the X (FTTx) includes various market types based on the specific location where the fiber optic cables terminate. Fiber to the Home/Premises/Building (FTTh/p/b) directly connects individual homes, premises, or buildings to the network. Fiber to the Node/Curb (FTTn/c) connects a larger group of homes or businesses to a central node or curb. Fiber to the Antenna (FTTa) brings fiber optic cables to cellular antennas for improved wireless connectivity. Each market type serves a different purpose in expanding high-speed internet access and connectivity.</p></p>
