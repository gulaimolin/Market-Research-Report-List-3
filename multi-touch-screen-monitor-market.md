<p><p>Multi-touch screen monitor market consists of three main types: capacitive, resistive, and sonic. Capacitive screens use electrodes to detect touch, offering smooth and accurate control. Resistive screens use two layers with a small gap, requiring pressure to register touch. Sonic screens utilize sound waves for touch detection, allowing for larger screens and improved durability. Each type has its own advantages and is suited for different applications based on factors such as sensitivity, accuracy, and durability.</p></p>
