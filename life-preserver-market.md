<p><p>Life preservers come in various types, including lifebelts, horseshoe lifebuoys, and survival floats. Lifebelts are designed to be worn around the waist and provide buoyancy in water emergencies. Horseshoe lifebuoys are shaped like a horseshoe and can be thrown to someone in distress. Survival floats are larger devices that can support multiple people in the water. Each type of life preserver serves a unique purpose in ensuring the safety of individuals in aquatic settings.</p></p>
