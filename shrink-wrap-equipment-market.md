<p><p>Shrink wrap equipment can be categorized into two main types based on the type of film used: PE Film Shrink Machines and POF Film Shrink Machines. PE Film Shrink Machines are designed to work with polyethylene film, which is suitable for packaging a wide range of products due to its flexibility and durability. On the other hand, POF Film Shrink Machines are specifically used with polyolefin film, which provides strong and secure wrapping for heavier or irregularly shaped items. Both types of equipment offer efficient and reliable packaging solutions for various industries.</p></p>
