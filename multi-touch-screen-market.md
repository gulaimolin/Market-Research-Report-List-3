<p><p>The multi-touch screen market can be categorized into different types based on the technology used. Capacitive technology relies on the electrical properties of the human body to track touch inputs accurately. Resistive technology uses pressure to detect touch points on the screen. Acoustic technology uses sound waves to recognize touch gestures. There are also other types of technologies being used in the market such as infrared and optical sensing. Each technology offers unique features and benefits to cater to various user needs and preferences.</p></p>
